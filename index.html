About Us

We are the leaders in interior fit out industry! We acquire your art, your style, your designs and your idea of an office or home and bring it back to you – Fulfilled.

'Khames Abdullah Said Co.’ is an Excellent Grade Company with core competence in construction, Interior fit-out and Maintenance. We strive to meet the expectations of our client through best Engineering Skill, a high degree of Professionalism and uncompromising Commitments. We have a Management with the unrelenting focus on Time, Cost and Quality aspects of the Projects. Since its inception in 1999, we have emerged with rich experience by successfully accomplishing various projects across the Sultanate and could garner the goodwill of an array of clients from a various domain such as Corporates, Public Sector Organizations, Hospitality industries, Educational Institutions etc...

Our Vision

At Khames Abdullah Said Co., we believe that design should be flexible to the needs of the client. We understand that our environment changes affects our way of life at home and how we do business. We make sure that our designs encompass these ideals. In our 15 years of existence as a company, we hold true to our mission statement "We care about your business." Our aim is to be the number one interior design company in the region.

Our Mission

Khames Abdullah Said Co. provide interior design service in such a way that it is both personal and cost-effective. Projects will be delivered on time and on budget with an unsurpassed attention to detail.

Message to Clients

Quality is at the heart of all our work, we care about the details because we know that our clients care about this too. Innovative work, superior fittings and finishing is critical in all our projects and this gives us great pride in the work that we do.

Our Services

We are offering a wide range of services from planning to build Projects, these are:

Engineering

We provide expert knowledge and services in Design Review, Risk Assessment, Review of supplier's proposals as well as Testing, Commissioning, Repair, Operation and Maintenance service..

Design

We worked with Corporate, Commercial, Hospitality & Retail Outlets. So, we know about interior space planning that is elegant and formal. Our designs for your office, hotel, restaurant and home are very practical, very modern and very distinctive.

False Ceiling

We are Specializing in Gypsum Decorative Ceiling & Gypsum Plain Ceiling, 60x60 False Ceiling, Aluminum Ceiling, Baffle Ceiling, Armstrong Dune Supreme Tile Ceiling, PVC Caoted Grid Ceiling and Wall décor & Interior design..

Partition

We are offering all kinds of Partitions and Renovation Services for the Residential and Commercial spaces such as Home, Office, Villas, Apartment, Retails, and Restaurants.

MEP

Supply, installation, testing and commissioning of complete MEP systems for all types of commercial, residential and industrial buildings and infrastructure project.

Wood & Joinery

We provide wooden ceiling, partition, wall panelling, skirting, receiption counter desk, wooden doors and windows, shlves, skirting and as well as kitchen counter

IT Solutions

We are extremely committed to delivering quality & support to assist our clients to achieve the maximum from their IT investment.

Interior Glass

We are specialist in supply & installation and maintenance for all kinds of glass works, aluminium works and mirror works.
